R-Ladies Irvine

About

R-Ladies Irvine is the Orange County, California chapter of R-Ladies Global, a worldwide organization dedicated to encouraging and promoting diversity in the R community. Our chapter supports members in their careers by offering networking opportunities, programming tutorials, and career direction at safe and inclusive events. Everyone is welcome to attend our events as long as they follow the R-Ladies Global Code of Conduct.

To stay in touch, you can join the SoCal R Users Slack workspace, our Meetup group, and our email list.

If you have any questions or would like to volunteer for R-Ladies Irvine, please contact us at irvine@rladies.org.
